Fanta X3D Model

This X3D model of this fanta can was created and animated in Cinema 4D, then exported as an FBX. The FBX was imported to 3ds Max and materials, cameras and lighting was added. It was then exported to VRML97 and converted, using the instantreality transcoders, to X3D for display online.
